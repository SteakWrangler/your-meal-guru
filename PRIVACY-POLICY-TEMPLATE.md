# Privacy Policy for Kitchen Companion

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

Kitchen Companion ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

**Recipe and Meal Data**
- Recipe searches and queries
- Meal plans you generate
- Dietary preferences and restrictions
- Ingredient lists you input
- Nutrition goals and tracking data

**Photos**
- Photos of ingredients you upload for analysis
- These photos are processed by our AI service and are not permanently stored on our servers
- Photos are temporarily held during processing (typically less than 60 seconds) and then deleted

**Chat History**
- Questions you ask our AI assistant
- Cooking-related conversations
- This data helps improve our AI responses

### Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data**
- Features you use most frequently
- Time spent in the app
- Navigation patterns
- Error logs and crash reports

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address (for security purposes)

**Location Data**
- We do NOT collect or track your location

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification documents
- Financial information (we don't sell anything yet)
- Health information (beyond dietary preferences)
- Contact lists
- Social media connections
- Precise location data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Generate personalized recipe suggestions
   - Create meal plans based on your preferences
   - Analyze ingredient photos
   - Provide nutrition tracking and analysis
   - Answer your cooking questions via AI chat

2. **Improve Our App**
   - Understand how features are used
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features
   - Improve AI accuracy

3. **Communicate with You**
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Send important app updates
   - Notify you of new features (if you opt in)

4. **Security and Fraud Prevention**
   - Detect and prevent abuse
   - Protect against spam and malicious activity
   - Maintain app security

## How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

### Service Providers

**Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)**
- Hosts our database and processes AI requests
- Subject to their privacy policy
- Uses industry-standard security practices

**AI Processing Services**
- Process recipe generation and ingredient recognition
- Data is anonymized when possible
- Not used for training without consent

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities

We will never share your data for marketing purposes without explicit consent.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Secure Storage**: Data stored on Supabase uses industry-standard security
- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits**: Security practices reviewed regularly

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

**Active Data**
- Your recipes, meal plans, and preferences are retained as long as you use the app
- Chat history retained for service improvement

**Temporary Data**
- Ingredient photos deleted within 60 seconds after processing
- AI processing cache cleared regularly

**Deleted Data**
- When you delete content, it's removed from our active systems within 30 days
- Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to say no to the sale of personal information

- Right to equal service and price

### European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights:
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Rights related to automated decision-making

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@yourmealguru.com
